The context

The real challenge of the mining sector

[EN] Camps, remote operations and temporary projects run in remote locations and under demanding conditions. The textile operation must hold up at a distance, without depending on nearby infrastructure or interrupting activity.

Camps

[EN] Bedding and workwear available for on-site personnel, with sustained replacement over time.

Remote operations

[EN] Reliable textile operations in remote locations, without depending on local infrastructure.

Temporary projects

[EN] Setup and teardown of the textile operation according to the duration and scale of each project.

Operational risks

What is at stake without control

  • [EN] Textile availability breakdowns at remote sites, with no nearby resupply.
  • [EN] Complex and costly logistics for remote and hard-to-reach environments.
  • [EN] Textile operations that do not withstand the demanding conditions of the environment.
  • [EN] Difficulty setting up and tearing down the operation in temporary projects.
  • [EN] Uncontrolled losses and replacements that raise operating costs.
What changes

What changes with the group's solution

[EN] The ecosystem coordinates specialized processing and operation to sustain on-site textiles in demanding and remote environments, with an operation that adapts to each project.

Standardized processing

[EN] Pacífica processes on-site textiles under a controlled standard, designed for operations that cannot stop.

Specialized operation

[EN] Operissa manages the textile operation on the ground, with people and processes adapted to the remote environment.

Sustained availability

[EN] The ecosystem ensures that workwear is available in remote locations, without breakdowns.
