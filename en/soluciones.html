Solution detail

Individual control of every textile asset

Each garment receives a unique identity that follows it throughout its entire life cycle: intake, use, washing, replacement and retirement. Operations stop managing batches and start managing assets.

Includes

  • Individual identification with long-life industrial RFID tags.
  • Reading infrastructure at key points of the operation.
  • Management platform with dashboards and KPIs.
  • Traceability by asset, area and wash cycle.
  • Loss control and intelligent inventories.
  • Operational auditing and internal compliance.
  • Integration with laundry and on-site operations.

Who it applies to

A capability designed for high-demand operations that need real textile traceability and control.

How it works

Four layers, one single data point

Traceability is not a device: it is a layered model that connects the physical asset with the operational decision.

  1. 1

    Identification

    Each textile asset receives an individual RFID tag with a unique identity.

  2. 2

    Reading

    Reading infrastructure automatically records every movement.

  3. 3

    Platform

    The data is consolidated into dashboards, KPIs and management analytics.

  4. 4

    Integration

    The information is integrated into laundry and on-site operations.
