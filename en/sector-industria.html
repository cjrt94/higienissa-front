The context

The real challenge of the industry sector

Textiles are part of regulatory compliance and workplace safety; without traceability there is no control over assignment, wash frequency, wear or compliance.

Regulatory compliance

Textiles are part of the operation's regulatory compliance and workplace safety.

Workplace safety

Uniforms and textile PPE assigned per worker must stay available and in usable condition.

Traceability

Without traceability there is no control over assignment, wash frequency, wear or compliance.

Operational risks

What is at stake without control

  • Improper use of uniforms and textile PPE.
  • Loss of uniforms.
  • Internal non-compliance.
  • Lack of traceability per worker.
What changes

What changes with the group's solution

The ecosystem connects operation and traceability to turn uniforms and textile PPE into worker-managed assets that are auditable and always available.

  • Digital assignment per employee.
  • Per-shift logging.
  • Wash / replacement control.
  • Auditable reports.
  • Logistics optimization.
Manageable indicators

What becomes measurable

  • Active uniforms per worker.
  • Rotation per shift.
  • Service life.
  • Internal compliance.
  • Replacement costs.
Impact by role

Safety / Compliance

Auditable reports and per-worker traceability to demonstrate internal compliance.

HR

Digital assignment per employee and wash and replacement control for each uniform.
