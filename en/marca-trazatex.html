Who we are

RFID technology, software, data and auditing for the textile asset

Trazatex is the intelligence and traceability division of Grupo Higienissa. We integrate RFID identification, management platforms and operational auditing so that every garment is visible, measurable and controllable throughout its useful life. That traceability generates real-time data —critical information for managing hospital, hospitality and industrial textiles.

How it works

The 4-layer model

From garment to data, and from data to operation: each layer enables the next.

1

Individual identification

RFID per garment; bulk reading without manual counting.

2

Reading infrastructure

RFID tunnels, cabins, handheld readers and stations adapted to the flow.

3

Management platform

Real-time visualization, control by area, historical reports, KPIs and digital auditing.

4

Operational integration

Model designed around each organization's real flow.

What information it generates

From every garment, a decision

Trazatex doesn't just identify garments: it produces the information that's missing today to manage textiles as an asset. Some of the data it generates:

Inventory by service

How many garments there are and where, broken down by service or area.

Consumption by floor

What each floor or unit consumes, to assign real costs.

Wash cycles per garment

How many times each individual garment has been washed.

Remaining useful life

How much each asset has left before replacement.

Lost garments

What's missing, since when and at which point in the flow.

Average rotation time

How long a garment takes to return to circulation.

Cost per use

The real cost of each garment spread across its uses.

Loss trend

How shrinkage evolves over time.
