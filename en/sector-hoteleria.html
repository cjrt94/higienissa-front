The context

The real challenge of the hospitality sector

High turnover of linen and bed-linen drives constant losses, preventive overbuying and inaccurate inventories. Without individual traceability, the spend builds up year after year as a hidden cost that no one manages to quantify.

  • There is no way to identify where garments are lost.
  • Real rotation by area is not tracked.
  • Replacement is not planned strategically.
  • Internal leakage is hard to detect.
Operational risks

What is at stake without control

Without textile control, both cost and the guest experience are left exposed.

  • Untraceable linen and bed-linen losses that raise replacement costs.
  • Textile availability gaps that disrupt room setup.
  • Textiles in poor condition that degrade guest experience and perception.
  • Housekeeping stalled by a lack of clean stock at the right moment.
  • Inability to measure consumption, rotation and textile lifespan by area.
What changes

What changes with the group's solution

The ecosystem brings together processing, traceability and operations to turn linen and bed-linen into a managed, available asset that serves the guest experience.

  • Control by location of each textile asset.
  • Focus on the critical areas with the highest rotation.
  • Optimized replacement and less dead inventory.
  • Accurate financial information to plan ahead.
  • Greater efficiency in housekeeping.
