The context

The real challenge of the hospitality sector

Constant losses, preventive overbuying, inaccurate inventories and internal leakage that is hard to pin down.

Hidden financial problem: Without traceability there is no way to know where the item is lost; the spend accumulates year after year.

Operational risks

What is at stake without control

  • Untraceable linen and bed-linen losses that raise replacement costs.
  • Textile availability gaps that disrupt room setup.
  • Textiles in poor condition that degrade guest experience and perception.
  • Housekeeping stalled by a lack of clean stock at the right moment.
  • Inability to measure consumption, rotation and textile lifespan by area.
What changes

What changes with the group's solution

The ecosystem brings together processing, traceability and operations to turn linen and bed-linen into a managed, available asset that serves the guest experience.

  • Control by location.
  • Focus on critical areas.
  • Optimized replacement.
  • Less dead inventory.
  • Accurate financial information.
Manageable indicators

What becomes measurable

  • Loss per month.
  • Rotation by floor / area.
  • Time in circulation.
  • Optimal inventory.
  • ROI.
